About Barangay San Juan

Barangay San Juan is a vibrant community located in the heart of the city. With a population of approximately 10,000 residents, we pride ourselves on maintaining peace, order, and unity among our constituents.

Our barangay was established in 1965 and has since grown into a model community with excellent public services, clean surroundings, and active citizen participation.

We offer various services to our residents including barangay clearance issuance, business permits, mediation services, and community development programs.
